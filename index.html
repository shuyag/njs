Safety, Always

Sheprd solves all the problems babysitters and schoolbuses can't handle alone.

  • Safe Driving Record

    Sheprd performs a detailed Massachusetts RMV driving record inspection; drivers must have at least 5 years U.S. driving experience.

  • Childcare Experience

    Sheprd drivers must have at least 2 years experience in childcare, and First Aid certification.

  • Real Time Tracking

    The Sheprd app allows parents to track child-journeys in real-time. On-board telematics devices enable precise geo-fencing and vehicle behavior monitoring to identify training opportunities. Because Sheprd manages its fleet directly, we don’t rely on cell phone app data to know exactly where our vehicles are.

  • Driver Background

    Sheprd drivers undergo a rigorous vetting process including, but not limited to, the state-mandated process for school bus drivers (7D-licensing). This process includes CORI/SORI reports, and in-person interviews.

  • Insurance

    Sheprd’s fleet of vehicles carry School Pupil Transport insurance and our drivers carry the appropriate licensure (7D) to drive vehicles insured in such a capacity. Unlike its competitors, Sheprd’s insurance is in full regulatory compliance.

  • Vehicle Safety

    Sheprd uses an exclusive fleet of consistently-inspected Land Rover LR4 and Discovery SUVs in pristine condition (aesthetic and mechanical); vehicles are 2-years old or newer and pass regular 7D-compliant vehicle inspections.
